About Us

Recognized by the government of India (DPIIT)

Remine India Private Limited is a Uttarakhand based private ltd. Company Registered at dated 06-Dec-2022 on Ministry of Corporate Affairs.

It has been classified as company limited by shares and is registered under Registrar of Companies Uttarakhand India. Authorized share capital of Remine India Private Limited is Rs. 10,00,000 and its paid up capital is Rs. 10,00,000. It aspire to serve in recycling activities across the India.
